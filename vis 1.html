Avatar the phenominon

The first Avatar movie follows Jake Sully, a Marine who traveled to the alien planet of Pandora and operates an “Avatar”, a genetically engineered body that allows the human race to interact with the alien Na’vi species sent by a corporation to exploit Pandora’s natural resources. But as Jake forms a bond with the Navi, he must decide whether he wishes to side with these people, Will remain loyal to his employers

Beyond the story, the film set a new standard for Visual Effects using both CGI and emercive VFX, along with that, the pioneered a virtual camera system that filmed fully digitally inside the digital world in real time. All these elements resulted in the film becoming the highest-grossing movie of all time, not only in the domestic market but internationally.
